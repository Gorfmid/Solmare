
# GALAXY OVERVIEW

## Type
Spiral galaxy with fragmented outer arms and several uncharted void regions.

## Structure
- Core Dominion Zone (densely controlled)
- Mid-Rim Expansion Zones (active colonization + conflict)
- Outer Rim (low infrastructure, high autonomy)
- Deep Void (unstable navigation, rare contact)

## FTL Travel Model
- Jump-drive based traversal using fixed calibration nodes
- Accuracy degrades in Outer Rim
- Deep Void jumps are probabilistic, not deterministic

## Strategic Reality
Control is not continuous; it is node-based. Empires do not hold space—they hold corridors between systems.
