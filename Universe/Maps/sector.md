
# BOOK ONE SECTOR: KESTRAN SPIRAL SECTOR

## Overview
A mid-rim sector recently reclassified as "strategically unstable" due to inconsistent sensor reporting and loss of long-range relay reliability.

## Key Characteristics
- 6 known star systems
- 2 contested systems
- 1 restricted research system
- 1 uncharted anomaly zone

## Political Status
Officially under Unified Fleet Authority, but multiple empires maintain "observation claims" and covert monitoring assets.

## Narrative Function
This sector is the proving ground for cadet command progression and the origin point of the Book One anomaly.
