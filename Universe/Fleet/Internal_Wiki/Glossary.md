## How to use

Working definitions for intranet search and watchstanding. When Edition language and field habit disagree, prefer the labeled field habit in the log and escalate taxonomy through Doctrine — do not invent a friendlier word because the annex coffee line prefers it.

Public wiki keeps soft language for Neutral Zone findings: provisional, unconfirmed, pending classification. Do not promote unconfirmed polities to named civilizational status here.

## Sensor possibility states

Describe **data quality**, not civilizational conclusion:

`confirmed` · `probable` · `degraded` · `corrupted` · `contradictory`

Preserve contradictions as operational signal. Collapsing them early is a professional failure mode. See [Communications](Communications.md)

## Anomaly stages

Describe **escalation of systemic disagreement** (keep distinct from sensor states):

| Stage | Definition |
|-------|------------|
| **0** | None detected |
| **1** | Localized observational contradiction |
| **2** | Multi-system disagreement (sensor vs command) |
| **3** | Recursive contradiction (systems disagree with themselves) |
| **4** | Structural reality instability (mapping integrity failure) |

Both sensor states and anomaly stages may apply simultaneously. Reporting must keep them labeled separately — [Reporting Standards](Reporting_Standards.md) · [Templates](Templates.md)

## Command & information terms

| Term | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| **Information Authority Rule** | Command valid only if current, consistent with local sensors, and not contradicted by independent systems — else local ship command |
| **field survival authority** | Under degraded comms, captains may override delayed / contradictory / ambiguous higher orders |
| **truth states** | Multiple simultaneous legitimate pictures during command fragmentation |
| **custody** | Documented chain of who touched packets / buffers |
| **observed vs inferred** | Required speech habit; do not mix unlabeled |

## Classification reminder (public)

Allowed on open pages: *provisional*, *unconfirmed*, *Neutral Zone corridor approach*, *anomaly*, *structured observation pending classification*.

Disallowed on open pages: naming unconfirmed polities as confirmed civilizations; treating provisional ink as empty *or* as settled neighbors.
