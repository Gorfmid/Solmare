## Purpose

Public intranet reference for **confirmed** contact status and Fleet-recognized polities / member-world structures. This page is deliberately conservative.

---

## Fleet Authority & member worlds

Unified Fleet Authority is the officer's home polity: multi-world membership under shared passage law, Relay Charter communications backbone, Founders' Week cultural synchronization.

Member worlds vary locally (food, festivals, accents) while sharing imperial thread. Local harvest festivals on agricultural belts are **not** foreign polity holidays — do not confuse naming. See `Universe/cultural_touchstones.md`.

## Neutral Zone findings (public stance)

As of current public wiki policy, Neutral Zone corridor observations that have not completed classification **must not** be listed here as confirmed civilizations.

Allowed public phrasing:

- *Unconfirmed structured observations in provisional volumes*  
- *Anomaly Stage assessments pending multi-office review*  
- *Contact protocol not triggered / first contact has not occurred* (when technically accurate)

Disallowed on this page: naming unconfirmed polities; declaring peer status; treating provisional ink as empty *or* as confirmed neighbors.

> **[CLASSIFIED — post-authorization]**  
> Restricted assessments regarding peer-scale industrial presence, passive observation geometries, and named opposing institutional structures exist in authorized Intelligence / Doctrine channels only. Do not mirror those names onto public Known Civilizations entries.

## How to propose an update

1. File observation under [Reporting Standards](Reporting_Standards.md)  
2. Route through Intelligence + Cartography + Doctrine classification working group  
3. Wait for public-language release  
4. Historical Office updates [Timeline](Timeline.md) if origin narrative is affected  

Coffee arguments in the Doctrine Annex do not constitute publication authority.

## Member-world texture (examples)

Verdant Line agricultural corridors, ocean-world stews at potlucks, Selene flatbreads, industrial districts that treat Second Sunrise foundry aurora as weather. Local color does not create separate sovereign entries on this page — they are Fleet Authority member texture.

## Pre-contact populations

Survey discoveries of inhabited pre-contact systems fall under Exploration Ethics and first-contact protocol — not automatic member integration and not automatic threat headers. See Academy cases ([Historical Incidents](Historical_Incidents.md)).
