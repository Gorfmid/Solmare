## Weapons by class (summary)

Lasers (fighters/corvettes) · Ion (ion fighters; battleships) · Missiles (frigates) · Plasma (cruisers/HC) · Photon (dreadnaughts/leviathans) · Disruptors (titans) · Carriers: defensive, fighter-centric.

Role logic beats "bigger gun wins." See [Ship Classes](Ship_Classes.md).

---

## Shields

Cruisers are the first class to use shields as standard. Shield matrix degradation is a logistics problem as much as a tactical one (AMBER-LIMITED territory). Full reset often needs yard time.

---

## Sensors & information warfare

Sensor states are never absolute. Expect jamming, deception, lag exploitation, and data poisoning as known conditions. Local command supersedes delayed external command under uncertainty.

Anomaly stages 0–4 track observational contradiction escalation. See [Glossary](Glossary.md).

---

## Propulsion & fuel (logistics view)

Fleet propulsion across classes is tracked as **fuel** / FME for resupply planning regardless of specific drive architecture. Fuel cannot be improvised the way spare parts sometimes can.

See [Logistics](Logistics.md)

---

## Scout systems character

Scouts emphasize range, observation, and self-sufficiency over combat dominance. Simpler local loops can be resilient under degraded information — **not immune**. Strength is interpretive clarity, not magic stealth.

---

## Simulation lag

Academy bridge simulators run Fleet OS versions with ~six-month lag. Field officers will meet newer interfaces before some cadets do. Brief accordingly.

---

## Fighter & carrier note

Fighters and ion fighters do not freestand logistically; their resupply is a carrier/base problem. See [Logistics](Logistics.md). Targeting data quality often matters more than airframe count — information warfare applies to fighter direction as much as capital sensors.

## Damage states officers misname

Do not call shield flicker "hull breach." Do not call emitter silence "reactor failure." Use Engineering vocabulary in logs: OW flags, matrix degradation, FME remaining, compartment seal status.

## Unknown technology rule

If you encounter systems that do not match Fleet class tables, report as unknown / provisional. Do not force a class label to comfort Doctrine. Canon consistency rule: Universe/canon_and_world_rules.md.
