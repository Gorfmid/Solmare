## Mission

FLC does not decide where the fleet goes. FLC determines **how long it can stay there**.

Mandate: fuel, materiel, maintenance scheduling, deployment sustainability, industrial monitoring.

> *"The fleet that runs out of fuel first loses. Everything else is commentary."*  
> — attributed, Academy plaque

---

## Network in one page

The logistics network is a **web with a spine**. The **fuel spine** chains high-capacity depots from Helion Prime through mid-rim toward frontier edge (teaching path: Helion → Althen → Caranth → Meric-7 → forward caches).

Beyond Meric-7, confidence degrades. Forward caches exist on paper; currency varies.

| Region | HQ (teaching) | Character |
|--------|---------------|-----------|
| Core Sector RLC | Helion Prime | Dense, redundant |
| Mid-Rim RLC | Station Caranth | Reliable primary; variable fallback |
| Frontier Adjacency RLC | Outpost Meric-7 | Understaffed relative to geography |

## Scout logistics truth

Scouts burn least fuel and operate farthest from Grade-A infrastructure. EDP ([LC-7](Forms.md)) is optimism with forms attached. Cache stock currency on Neutral Zone approaches is a known soft number.

When Intelligence returns foreign facility observations, FLC asks for **rate, duration, and tonnage**. Capability timelines are FLC's grammar; intent debates are someone else's.

## Culture

Coffee. Always. Charts and schedules on walls, not art. *"Tell me the number. Tell me the trend. Then you can have a conclusion."*

## Node types (quick)

Yard Facility · Relay Depot · Fuel Station · Mobile Tender · Forward Cache · Emergency Reserve  

Redundancy doctrine: two independent supply paths inside reliable network range. Frontier edge routinely accepts single-path under EDP — permitted, not recommended.

## What gets softened (read footnotes)

OW backlog reported as aggregate; cache currency as estimate-at-last-confirm; sustainment windows as maxima assuming no deviation. FLC reports accurately and still expects you to read the appendix.

## Career note

FLC evaluates **accuracy of projection**. Optimistic lies end careers faster than pessimistic plans that work. Depot rotations teach what simulations clean.
