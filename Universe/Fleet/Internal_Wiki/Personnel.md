---

## Who this page covers

Commissioned officers, warrant officers, enlisted ratings, and authorized civilian specialists assigned to Fleet ships and shore commands. Member-world defense exchanges are rare; see Academy Joint Service Exchange notes in [Fleet Academy](Fleet_Academy.md).

---

## Career spine (typical)

1. Academy candidacy → commissioning ([Fleet Academy](Fleet_Academy.md))  
2. First posting (often division / watch / depot)  
3. Specialty deepening ([Training](Training.md))  
4. Department head / staff billet  
5. Command track or shore institutional track  

Promotion boards see **cohort** performance (Founders' Week cohort naming) as well as individual records. Major-Cycle cohorts carry social identity across careers.

---

## Assignment culture

- Frontier patrol, scout-track, and exploration billets are high-prestige among cadets; officers who have lived them sometimes reconsider.
- Logistics and administrative billets keep the fleet moving; undervaluation is a known cultural failure mode.
- Instructors rotate through Academy postings so faculty does not forget fleet service.

Posting ceremony (Year 4) precedes sword ceremony by ~ten weeks. See Academy reference for ritual detail.

---

## Civil-military interface billets

Some personnel are formally paid by one division while borrowed by another (example pattern: Cartography pay while supporting Intelligence pattern work). This is normal interface practice — not a paperwork error. Document the borrow on the assignment order.

See [Fleet Intelligence](Fleet_Intelligence.md)

## Conduct expectations (short)

- Carry truth of observation; report honestly (Commissioning Oath language)
- Protect civilian life and authority in operations
- Exercise judgment when orders do not arrive
- Do not invent classification to avoid paperwork; do not under-classify to win arguments

Full regulatory spine: [Fleet Regulations](Fleet_Regulations.md)

---

## Leave, Founders' Week, and ordinary life

Founders' Week leave windows are synchronized empire-wide (local calendars adjust). Neighbor potluck customs and fortune rolls appear in civilian and Academy contexts. Officers on watch still get coffee; ceremony does not cancel the fuel spine.

---

## When personnel become witnesses

Anomalous contact, extended Neutral Zone observation, or survival under degraded doctrine can turn ordinary crews into **witnesses** rather than heroes. Historical Office and Doctrine Bureau treat these differently. Field officers should follow [Reporting Standards](Reporting_Standards.md) and resist narrative pressure until classification settles.

## Enlisted & warrant notes

This wiki skews officer because intranet authorship does. Warrant technical authority in engineering, communications, and navigation specialties is real — commissioned ego that ignores warrants produces incidents.

Enlisted ratings run the watches that make officer decisions possible. Department pages apply to their work centers.

## Assignment preference vs Fleet need

Cadets cheer frontier postings. Fleet needs logistics officers. Both statements are true. Wellhaven alumni will remind Helion alumni how often captains lose ships to supply failure.
