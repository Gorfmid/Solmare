# Medical

**Related:** [Personnel](Personnel.md) · [Ships](Ships.md) · [Scout Operations](Scout_Operations.md) · [Operational Procedures](Operational_Procedures.md)

---

## Function

Shipboard Medical maintains crew fitness for duty, manages casualty response, quarantine, and extended-deployment psychological readiness. Shore medical facilities (Academy Meridian Hall and regional hospitals) handle Tier-depth care beyond sickbay.

On scout hulls, Medical is often a single officer or dual-hat billet. That does not shrink the responsibility.

---

## Readiness interface

Medical can ground crew and, through command, constrain deployment. Logistics readiness codes are hull-centric; Medical adds the human constraint. A GREEN hull with RED crew is not deployable.

Extended Deployment Protocol assumes crew resilience as a planning input. Medical briefings before EDP departures are mandatory on paper and frequently compressed in practice — do not skip the psych check because the coffee line is long.

---

## Emergency return priority

On ERP intake ([Logistics](Logistics.md)):

1. Life safety / medical evaluation (0–4 hours)  
2. Strategic data custody (if scout/intel vessel)  
3. Mission continuation assessment  
4. Hull triage  

Medical does not own data custody but can block interviews until patients are stable. Document the block.

---

## Quarantine & contact medicine

First-contact and anomaly scenarios may trigger medical holds independent of Doctrine headers. Follow Edition contact medical annexes when issued; when not issued, escalate rather than invent protocol.

Public wiki: do not publish speculative pathogen notes for Neutral Zone observations. Route classified medical findings through authorized channels.

---

## Culture

Sickbay coffee is terrible. Patients still ask for it. Founders' Week leave medical staffing is a known surge — Historical Office exhibitions do not count as medical justification for skipping clinic hours.

---

## Extended-deployment medical checklist (digest)

Before EDP departure, Medical should confirm:

1. Crew fitness-for-duty letters current  
2. Psych readiness notes filed (even if compressed)  
3. Controlled med inventory against mission duration + margin  
4. Quarantine kit seals intact  
5. Interview-hold authority understood by CO/XO  

On return under ERP, Medical may delay Intelligence interviews. Stamp the hold; do not negotiate it verbally only.

## Shore vs ship sickbay

Academy Meridian Hall and regional hospitals handle what scout sickbays cannot. Transfer criteria: surgical need, extended psych hold, pathogen unknowns. Pathogen speculation for provisional corridor observations stays classified until Medical + Doctrine release language — see [Known Civilizations](Known_Civilizations.md).

## Founders' Week surge

Clinic traffic rises during Founders' Week leave. Plan watch bills accordingly. Ceremony is not a medical waiver.
