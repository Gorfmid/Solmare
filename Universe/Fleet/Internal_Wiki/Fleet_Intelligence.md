## Function

Synthesize quiet reports into strategic picture — pattern across boring packets, not only dramatic intercepts. Intelligence collects frontier assessment, analyzes threat patterns, and submits classified strategic reports to Military Command.

**Critical limitation:** Intelligence has **no enforcement authority**. Value depends on successful delivery, interpretation, and approval inside the command hierarchy. Reports can be delayed, buried, or lost without immediate systemic awareness.

---

## Cultural personality

- Analytical; structured lists under stress  
- Pattern hunters who preserve contradictions instead of collapsing them early  
- Emotionally controlled briefing style  
- Threat-first habit that must be restrained by evidence standards  
- Respect for Cartography conditionals (*observed* vs *inferred*)

Coffee is present in assessment rooms. Numbers arrive before adjectives.

## Uncertainty quantification

Academy Intel Track grades Year 4 practica on **quality of uncertainty**, not false confidence. Field reports that overclaim from ambiguous evidence are institutional hazards.

Use [Reporting Standards](Reporting_Standards.md) language: observed / probable / degraded / corrupted / contradictory.
