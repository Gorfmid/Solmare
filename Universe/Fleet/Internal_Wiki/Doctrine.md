## Function

Edition changes are political events. Wording is negotiated.

## Expansion vs security (taught debate)

Orthodox expansion doctrine: exploration creates stability; open passage creates peace. Security doctrine minority: buffer space has value; unknown corridors carry risk exploration alone does not resolve; Neutral Zone existence implies awareness of something beyond it.

Cadets who hear only one side have not been taught. Field officers who pretend the debate does not exist will meet it in coffee lines after anomalous returns.

## Boxes that fail

When observation does not fit UNKNOWN CONTACT templates, do not hammer the observation until it fits. File limitations. Thessaly-style terminology papers are not insubordination by default — they are data about language.

Public wiki keeps Edition categories until public successor language issues.
