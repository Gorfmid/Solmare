## What this page is

A digest of **standing procedures** officers are expected to execute without opening the full manuals. It is not a substitute for ship standing orders, mission annexes, or Edition primers. When local orders conflict with this digest, follow the more specific lawful order — then file the conflict.

Coffee may be present on the desk. It is not a procedure clock. Use stamps, watch changes, and form versions.

## Command under normal and degraded conditions

**Normal chain:** Civilian political authority → Military Command → Task Forces → Captains.

**Degraded communications / combat:** Captains may override delayed, contradictory, or ambiguous higher orders. Field survival supersedes hierarchical delay. This is designed tolerance, not romantic independence.

**Information Authority Rule:** A command is valid only if it is verifiably current, consistent with local sensors, and not contradicted by independent systems. If any condition fails, authority defaults to local ship command.

See [Operations](Operations.md) · [Communications](Communications.md)

## Mission posture & sustainment windows

Posture types used in ops–logistics interface: ROUTINE PATROL · EXTENDED PATROL · SURGE OPERATION · CRISIS OPERATION.

Before you accept a window that looks heroic on a briefing slide, confirm fuel state, cache currency, and abort thresholds with Logistics language. Scouts and frontier frigates commonly require Extended Deployment Protocol authorization — Form [LC-7](Forms.md). Dropping below EDP fuel floors without confirmed cache is a procedure failure, not a morale story.

## Observation & contact baseline

Edition 143 teaching baseline for managed contact corridors:

1. Observe before hail  
2. Complete the required observation passes (scout culture: three full passes where protocol applies)  
3. Preserve sensor contradictions — do not collapse multi-system disagreement into a tidy cause  
4. Label *observed* vs *inferred*  
5. Escalate civilizational implications before irreversible action  

Public ops language for Neutral Zone corridor approaches remains provisional: *unconfirmed*, *structured observation pending classification*, *anomaly*. Do not drive public ROE changes from rumor, wardroom consensus, or coffee-line certainty.

> **[CLASSIFIED — post-authorization]**  
> Restricted corridor contact variants, segment-level packages, and readiness-surge routing use authorized channels only. Do not paste restricted annexes into ship public folders.

See [Scout Operations](Scout_Operations.md) · [Known Civilizations](Known_Civilizations.md) · [Doctrine](Doctrine.md)

## Emission & custody discipline

Active illumination near corridor approaches is a **command event**, not a habit. Passive listen / emitter state belongs in the log with timestamps. Custody cover sheets exist because adjectives do not survive peer review.

If you cannot prove who touched the buffer, you do not have a conclusion — you have a well-formatted rumor. Duplicate under uncertain integrity; never assume real-time strategic guidance on frontier scout ops.

## Damage control, compartmentalization, and abort

Ships are layered defensive environments. Section seals, local loops, and abort thresholds exist because optimism is not a fuel state and heroism is not a damage-control plan. Captains set local drill calendars; shore audits exist. See [Engineering](Engineering.md) · [Training](Training.md)

Emergency return sequences prioritize fuel, medical intake, data custody, and debrief routing — [Logistics](Logistics.md) ERP family.

## Crisis fragmentation (expect it)

In Fleet-scale conflict, political authority becomes delayed or symbolic, military command partially blind, and field command primary. Multiple legitimate “truth states” and conflicting lawful orders are a **known condition**. Document disagreements; do not erase them to make the summary slide pretty.

People before systems on live calls: ask who is on the deck before what the deck shows.

## When procedure ends

Protocol takes you to the edge. At the edge, you are the protocol — Academy line, still operationally true. File the decision chain honestly. Prefer witness framing over hero framing when events exceed curriculum.

## Contradictory orders checklist

1. Verify timestamps and channel authenticity  
2. Compare to local sensors  
3. Note which systems contradict  
4. Choose under Information Authority Rule  
5. Log the truth state you acted under  
6. Notify higher command when able — do not wait forever to survive  

## Civilian protection

Oath and Edition force obligations stand even when frontier adrenaline argues otherwise. Record civilian contacts; do not treat them as scenery.
