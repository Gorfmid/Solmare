## Founding-era standardization

### Vellor Discrepancy

Two Fleet vessels handled frontier contact differently in the same corridor within four months. Both captains believed they were following doctrine — and were, under the doctrine each had learned. Taught as the founding argument for Academy standardization, not as cartoon failure. Simulation pair: *The Vellor Pair*.
