## What this page is

A **spine summary** for daily compliance — not the full regulatory corpus. When conflict exists, Edition primer + issued ROE + ship standing orders control; then escalate.

---

## Regulatory layers

1. **Civilian political law** — trade, diplomacy, member-world authority, funding oversight  
2. **Fleet Military regulations** — deployments, force authorization, doctrine enforcement  
3. **Ship standing orders** — captain's local implementation  
4. **Mission ROE / annexes** — corridor- and task-specific  

Governance model: `Universe/Fleet/fleet_authority_governance_and_command_hierarchy.md`

---

## Non-negotiables officers recite

- Protect civilian life and authority in operations (oath language)  
- Report observations honestly  
- Do not treat delayed or contradictory higher orders as automatically binding under degraded information conditions — apply Information Authority Rule  
- Do not under- or over-classify for convenience  
- EDP abort thresholds and fuel emergency declarations are safety regulations, not suggestions  

---

## Honor & conduct

Academy Honor Code continues in spirit after commissioning. Interpersonal friction notes, false reporting, and custody tampering are career-ending faster than honest error.

---

## Force & engagement

Use of force follows Edition engagement sections and issued ROE. Risk-averse engagement policy for scouts is intentional. Scouts are not authorized to invent capital-ship bravado.

See [Doctrine](Doctrine.md) · [Scout Operations](Scout_Operations.md) · combat logic doc.
