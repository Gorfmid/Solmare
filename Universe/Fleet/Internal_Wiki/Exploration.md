## Function

Exploration Corps puts Fleet ideology into motion: the unknown exists to be explored; patrols gather data for expansion and safety; scouts are eyes, not the battle line.

Routine reconnaissance is the job. Folklore attaches to aging scout hulls whether Command likes it or not.

---

## Cultural personality

- Optimistic professional curiosity  
- First-arrival identity (*Someone has to go first*)  
- Respect for craft (correction thrusters, emitter discipline) when survival teaches it  
- Discomfort when passive-only doctrine becomes the whole product  
- Young institution-within-institution energy

Coffee on survey decks; Founders' Week programs on mess walls; charts with provisional ink.

---

## Relationship to Doctrine

Exploration champions expansion doctrine. Security doctrine advocates (Academy minority voice) argue buffer value and risk in unknown corridors. Both appear in training. Field officers must execute the edition they are issued while filing observations that may break it.

See [Doctrine](Doctrine.md)

---

## When "first" is late

If observation suggests prior civilizational infrastructure in a provisional volume, Exploration's identity takes a hit. Report the observation without rewriting it into hero narrative or cowardice narrative.

Public language remains provisional / anomaly / pending classification. See [Known Civilizations](Known_Civilizations.md) · [Reporting Standards](Reporting_Standards.md)

---

## Career notes

Scout Track cadets feed Corps billets. Prestige is real; cache currency is also real. Read [Logistics](Logistics.md) before romanticizing the edge.

---

## Corps vs Cartography vs Intelligence

Exploration wants to *see*. Cartography wants notation discipline. Intelligence wants pattern without premature headers. Healthy missions keep all three in the coffee line.

When Corps liaison demands active return missions before Fleet Command approves, the correct answer is the issued ROE — not wardroom courage.

## First-arrival identity under stress

If logs suggest Fleet arrived late to a volume, do not rewrite the proverb. Rewrite the report. Identity crisis is Academy/Doctrine territory after custody is secure.
