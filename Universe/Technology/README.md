## Usage rule

Before inventing or describing technology in prose:

1. Check **`Technology_Bible.md`** for what the system **can and cannot** do.
2. Prefer failure, degradation, and crew judgment over capability spectacle.
3. Do not contradict Book One, scout-class limits, or communication/sensor doctrine.
4. Treat Fold as a separate anomaly domain — not a standard propulsion upgrade.
