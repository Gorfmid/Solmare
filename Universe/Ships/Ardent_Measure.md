## 1. Core Identity

The **Ardent Measure** is a **newer** Fleet scout-class hull on ordinary Segment patrol rotation — not a prestige posting, not a legend.

| Field | Lock |
|-------|------|
| **Class** | Scout-class reconnaissance (14 billets standard) |
| **Age of service** | Young relative to *Kestrel Veil* (*Veil* = forty-plus years, patched forever). *Ardent Measure* is recent-build / mid-single-digit to low-teens years in service — still within original systems generations, not a museum of workarounds. Exact commission year: **open** until needed. |
| **Mission texture (pre-Ch. 6)** | Routine Segment 6 patrol: systems checks, training rotations, sensor calibration, quiet returns. A successful week was one nobody remembered. |
| **Ch. 6 posture** | Pulled off routine patrol onto **elevated observation** / Orchard Compact **watch support** under Calder's field judgment. Passive preferred. No illumination without local survival need. |

**Contrast with *Kestrel Veil*:** same class, different biography. *Veil* carries scars, Incident memory, and crew folklore. *Ardent Measure* carries clean couplers, recent yard time, and a crew that expected boredom. Ch. 6 is the first time the hull's history feels like it might matter.

**Do not:** make her a second *Veil* (no Corridor C kick mythos, no "old girl" voice). **Do:** show competence, speed, and the shock of a quiet Segment craft sent toward something that already destroyed a colony.

## 3. Physical Condition (Book Two Ch. 6)

- Scout-class baseline: fast, small, light tactical, long passive observation.
- Pre-divert: routine patrol maintenance window (three days earlier on schedule).
- Yard Three: berth lights stand-down amber → elevated observation white (same white as *Quiet Ledger*).
- Underway: no ceremony, no formation escort; sealed mission updates and revised route only.
- Coupler / maintenance work in progress at alert (see Kade). Grease-print checklist accepted — perfect paperwork deprioritized.

**Open / do not invent yet:** specific Reing quirks, scar locations, unique door rituals. Reveal through failure or maintenance only if a scene needs it.
