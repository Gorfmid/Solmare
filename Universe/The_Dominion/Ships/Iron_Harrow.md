## 1. Identity

**Iron Harrow** — Leviathan-class warship assigned to Commander Vex for the classified cloak field trial and subsequent V17 standing observation.

- **Class** remains **Leviathan** in institutional and technical documentation.
- **Name** is Dominion command usage — not known to Fleet at Book One Ch. 22 close.
- **Tone:** dark-running, preparation, strategic depth — not spectacle.

**Naming note:** *Harrow* — depth, pressure, furrow; *Iron* — yard inheritance, refusal to bend. Dominion warship names tend toward material and consequence, not hero epithets.
