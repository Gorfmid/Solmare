# BOOK ONE OUTLINE

## Title
*The Kestrel Veil Incident* (The Solmare Cycle · Book One)

## Core Premise
A newly commissioned captain inherits an aging scout on routine patrol. What appears ordinary becomes the **Kestrel Veil Incident** — the defining contact event of Book One — then discovery of a structured opposing civilization beyond the border, then the Fleet's first doctrine shift in response.

## 4-Act Architecture

| Act | Ch | Arc |
|-----|-----|-----|
| I — Routine Patrol | 1–4 | Empire, crew, ship, mission begins |
| II — The Kestrel Veil Incident | 5–8 | Contact, survival, recovery, return *(locked)* |
| III — Shadows Beyond the Border | 9–16 | Unknown operational civilization — border intelligence |
| IV — First Doctrine | 17–24 | Strategic assessment and doctrine formation |

## Key Escalation
Routine patrol → **Incident** (Ch. 5–8) → border discovery (Ch. 9–16) → Fleet doctrine shift (Ch. 17–24)

## Canonical Framing
- Escalation of **understanding**, not episodic mystery
- Enemy = **expanding structured presence**, not anomaly system
- Legacy Fold / living map / reality-fracture arc **superseded**

## Ending (Ch. 24 — The Veil Opens)
Book One fully resolved. Fleet permanently changed by Incident and border intelligence. Strategic truth established at operational level. Sequel hook from **consequences**, not withheld mystery.
