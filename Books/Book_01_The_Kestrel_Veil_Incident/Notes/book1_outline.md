
# BOOK ONE OUTLINE

## Title (Placeholder)
"Edge of the Helion Drift"

## Core Premise
A newly promoted cadet is given command of a scout vessel on a routine reconnaissance mission into a politically unstable sector, only to discover that the data integrity of known space is failing.

## Key Escalation
Scout mission → anomaly detection → communication degradation → isolated survival decisions → forced autonomy → promotion

## Ending
Successful mission completion with partial data recovery and first confirmed anomaly event classified as non-random systemic failure.
