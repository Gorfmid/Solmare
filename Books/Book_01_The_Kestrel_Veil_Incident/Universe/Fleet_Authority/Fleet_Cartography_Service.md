# Fleet Cartography Service
## Survey, Charts, and Frontier Notation — Unified Fleet Authority

---

## 1. Function

Fleet Cartography maps the quadrant Fleet believes it is stewarding— survey epochs, frontier provisional volumes, lane merges, relay installs, *empty* notation.

Cartography produces the paper reality Fleet operates on:

- Patrol routes
- Neutral Zone corridor charts (seven-alpha among them)
- VI-Gamma-class watchpost reference frames
- Helion Prime dispatch geometry

**Dana Holt** is Cartography culture in miniature— nineteen years of shifts, procedural grammar, *observed vs inferred*.

---

## 2. Cultural Personality

- **Patient.** Will not rush a label (Holt ethos).
- **Scientific under paperwork.** Survey metadata matters; supervisors think it's boring until it isn't.
- **Epoch pride.** New install = new truth; older bands overwritten reluctantly.
- **Underfunded fringe posts.** VI-Gamma is underused posting— competent, isolated, essential.
- **Believes maps are owned.** Fleet borders are lines Fleet drew.

---

## 3. Act IV Relevance

Thessaly/Reyes finding: **Fleet borders exist mainly on Fleet maps.**

Cartography must respond to:

- Lane antiquity contradicting frontier provisional shading
- Industrial throughput in volumes marked *low activity*
- Holt/Nand chain as **proof of scout continuity** and bearing truth
- Ch. 17 *Listening Post* elevation of Cartography nodes as strategic instruments

Act IV makes Cartography a **battleground of notation**, not starships.
