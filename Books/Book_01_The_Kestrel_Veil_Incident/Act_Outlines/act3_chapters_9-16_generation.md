## Act III Function

**Discovery of an unknown operational civilization** — not mystery-anomaly episodes.

Book One escalates **understanding**:

- Act II (Ch. 5–8) = the Incident — crew survives structured contact
- **Act III (Ch. 9–16)** = border intelligence — crew discovers organized opposing presence beyond the border
- Act IV (Ch. 17–24) = doctrine formation — Fleet strategic reckoning

The enemy is an **expanding structured presence**, not a mystery anomaly system.
