# Book Two Master Outline

**Theme:** Trust is Harder Than War  
**Time gap:** ~18 months after Book One  
**Target:** ~180,000 words  
**Rule:** Outline / planning only — no chapter prose  
**Peligar seed:** `Universe/The_Dominion/peligar_planning_seed.md`

## Structure

- **Prelude** — The Founding Exodus
- **Act I (Ch. 1–4)** — Uneasy Neighbors
- **Act II (Ch. 5–8)** — The Burning Fringe
- **Act III (Ch. 9–13)** — The First Accord
- **Act IV (Ch. 14–18)** — The Expanding Shadow
- **Act V (Ch. 19–22)** — The Cost of Trust
- **Act VI (Ch. 23–25)** — The Helion Accord

---

## Major Story Beats

1. **Prelude:** Founding exodus from Dominion territory during harvest festival — later becomes Founders' Week. Reader knows; characters do not.
2. **Act I open:** Peligar fire on *Iron Harrow*; Fleet/*Kestrel* intercepts baffling multi-vocal "screaming" and has no category for the attacker.
3. **Act I:** Fragile Fleet–Dominion communications; Dominion states they need help against an expanding continuity civilization Fleet does not understand.
4. **Act I close:** A Fleet frontier colony (**Founders' Reach**, default) is processed/consumed — proof, not debate. Act ends on institutional shock.
5. **Act II:** Fallout, scale recognition, casualty reckoning, political necessity — Fleet begins considering military cooperation. Not first discovery of the threat.
6. **Acts III–VI:** Joint patrols → expanding shadow → failed joint ops / trust tests → limited Helion Accord (rescue rights, warning network, joint observation — **no formal alliance**). Ellis seeds shared-origin discovery; Calder/Vex professional trust.
