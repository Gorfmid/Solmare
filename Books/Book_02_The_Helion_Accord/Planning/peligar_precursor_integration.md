- Book One Peligar presence ≠ attack fleet / invasion force
- Book One anomalies around *Kestrel Veil* caused **in part** by precursor activity
- Slow expansion: identify → long-term assess → evaluate conditions → decide processing viability
- Not recon / war / diplomacy in their frame — **assessment**
- Arrival ≠ invasion; arrival = evaluating whether a region is worth consuming
- Process-driven; not empire; not territory conquest; no human motives
- Hulls = obstacles / variables / environmental conditions
- *Veil* = first identifiable **witness**, not the anomaly itself
- Dominion endured, did not defeat; later terror: **they never left — they have been assessing**
