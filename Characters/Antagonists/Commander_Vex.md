## THE CLOAKING FIELD (CANON)

- **Prototype / sole installation** on Vex's Leviathan-class command — experimental, not fleet-wide.
- Designed to maintain full masking envelope in contested space.
- **Failure mode (Ch. 5):** envelope could not hold under sustained scout-class passive lock; collapse window aligned with passive lock confirmation, not active targeting.
- Vex's operational requirement: *"It must maintain."* — treated as non-negotiable capability, not optional upgrade.
- Post-contact action: internal failure marked; field rebuild ordered before continued operations.

**Do not** imply other Leviathan-class hulls share this cloak until outline establishes rollout.
