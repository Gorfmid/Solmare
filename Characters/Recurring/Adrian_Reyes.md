# ADRIAN REYES — ACADEMY CLASSMATE (BOOK ONE, CH. 1–2)

**Status:** Active (off-page after Ch. 2)  
**Role:** Newly commissioned officer; exploration cartography track  
**Age:** ~24 (Founders' Week graduation cohort)  
**Posting:** UFA Scout Vessel *Meridian Echo* (assignment day, Ch. 2)

---

## STORY FUNCTION

Calder's **academy peer**—warmth and competence at graduation, not a shipboard character. Keeps Calder from being socially isolated during Founders' Week while **Damon Reyes** on *Kestrel Veil* remains a seasoned stranger with the same surname.

**Reader should feel:** Calder has friends who earned respectable postings; the name collision on the bridge is a joke, not a reunion.
