## HOW SHE INTERPRETS UNCERTAINTY

- Holds **dual hypotheses** until one breaks (e.g. equipment fault vs. contact behavior)
- Speaks in conditional grammar: *if this holds*, *when we see it again*
- Uncomfortable with silence from subordinates—reads silence as data
- Final reports distinguish **observed** from **inferred** even when Fleet prefers single-line summaries

**Dialogue:** Complete sentences shortened under fatigue; questions that are really checks. *"Say it again."* *"What did we not see?"*

**Never sounds like:** Bridge combat crew, Vex debrief coldness, political speech.
