# SCOUT SHIP CREW ROLES

**Book Two+ *Kestrel Veil* instance:** `KESTREL_VEIL_CREW_REORGANIZATION.md`

## Captain
Command authority; decision bottleneck; institutional accountability.

## Executive Officer (XO)
Operational voice of caution; prioritizes crew survival over mission completion. Watch-captain rhythm; doctrine translator.

## Chief Engineer
Maintains ship integrity; improvisation under damage; certification of what the hull can actually do.

**Book One instance:** Elara Koss  
**Book Two+ instance (*Veil*):** Tomas Brenner (Koss transfers to Interface Construction & Integration)

## Senior Navigator / Bridge Operations
Jump calculations, sensor interpretation, anomaly detection, tactical positioning, operational planning, command continuity on the bridge.

**Book One / Book Two+ instance:** Damon Reyes (expanded bridge responsibility Book Two+)

## Tactical & Defensive Systems
Threat assessment, engagement-rule interpretation, evasive protocol, tactical systems readiness. On scout-class vessels: survival geometry, not combat dominance.

**Book One instance:** Ari Halden  
**Book Two+ instance (*Veil*):** Marcus Hale (Halden transfers to Interface security architecture)

## Flight Operations
Shuttles, probes, launches, recoveries, local traffic; jump sequencing support under navigator.

**Book Two+ protected lead:** Yuki Tanaka

## Communications / Relay Operations
Delayed/fragmented fleet contact; operational messaging; may receive conflicting orders.

**Book One instance (ship):** Jun Park  
**Book Two+ instance (ship ops):** Jun Park (stays *Veil*); Nadia Cole → Interface station relay

## Chief Medical Officer
Crew health, fitness, rest cycles, emergency stabilization.

**Instance:** Dr. Seru Okwelu

## Life Support & Environmental
Atmosphere, water, berth environmental systems.

**Book Two+ protected lead:** Sana Mehta (reports to Chief Engineer)
