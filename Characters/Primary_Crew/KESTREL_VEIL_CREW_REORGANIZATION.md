## Interface Project

### Elara Koss

**Former role:** Lieutenant Commander; Chief Engineer, *Kestrel Veil* (Book One).

**Transfer:** Interface Station project — Chief Construction & Integration.

**Rank (locked):** Promoted **Lieutenant Commander → Commander** at Interface greenlight (Book Two Ch. 4).

**Role:** Construction management, systems integration, yard coordination, schedules, contractors, inspections, and practical execution of Brenner’s station concept.

Works with Brenner during construction. Later becomes one of Interface’s senior permanent officers.

### Ari Halden

**Former role:** Lieutenant Commander; Tactical Systems Officer, *Kestrel Veil* (Book One).

**Transfer:** Interface Project — Station Security.

**Rank (locked):** Promoted **Lieutenant Commander → Commander** at Interface greenlight (Book Two Ch. 4).

**Role:** Station defenses, security systems, emergency response architecture, sensors, internal security, and coordination with Walsh.

**Name collision lock:** Book Two institutional **Commander Hale** (Doctrine Bureau) ≠ **Marcus Hale** (*Veil* defensive/tactical). After promotion, Halden is also addressed as Commander — do not confuse the two.

### Brenner (does not leave *Kestrel Veil*)

Brenner becomes **Chief Engineer of *Kestrel Veil***, taking Koss’s former shipboard position.

He remains the **originator and principal engineering mind** behind the Interface concept while Koss handles much of construction and integration execution.

**Do not** accidentally make Brenner the full-time Interface station commander or remove him from *Kestrel Veil* unless a later explicit canon decision does so.

### Nadia Cole (Interface station relay)

**Former role:** Relay technician under Park, *Kestrel Veil* (Book One).

**Transfer:** Interface Project — station relay / communications.

**Role:** Station relay buildout and day-to-day Fleet relay once Interface has walls. Becomes **Dana Holt’s** primary Fleet counterpart on the station/node side.

**Do not** move Park off *Veil* day-to-day comms to make room for Cole. Park stays ship-side.

### Marcus Hale (ship tactical after Halden transfers)

While Halden is still aboard, Hale’s defensive work sits under Halden’s tactical authority.

After Halden transfers to Interface, **Hale is principal defensive/tactical systems aboard *Veil*** (Calder’s shipboard tactical advisor). Rank at Ch.4 greenlight: **Petty Officer Second Class → Petty Officer First Class** (one step). Not a jump to Halden’s former Lieutenant Commander — further rank earned later. He may still coordinate with Halden when ship systems or Interface security design touch.
