## ROLE

Ship Security and Access — Walsh's former seat. Cargo checks, hatch authority, crew systems enforcement, overflow work nobody else has time for. Also enforces Okwelu's medical / rest board aboard ship.

Works closely with Dr. Okwelu on crew wellness compliance. Reports to Thessaly. On a hatch dispute between Thessaly and Calder, takes Calder's order — then tells Thessaly immediately after.

Thessaly asked for him. Calder didn't want him. Thessaly talked Calder into it.

---

## BACKGROUND

Solis came up through Survey Detachment Seven — the kind of posting that rewards people who answer the question asked and don't improvise past their authority. He used "initiative" once in his interview with Calder. He used "proactive" once. Calder marked both.

He arrived at the exact scheduled time. Sat straight. Hands still. Did not ask about the yard that had just taken four officers from the ship — a silence Calder noted as awareness, not indifference.

His first ask after accepting the billet: a current access map.

He learns corridors before policing them. That is what Thessaly wanted.

---

## PRESENCE

Quiet, orderly, contained. Not flashy. Does not offer opinions until asked, and when asked, stays within the scope of the question.

He is finding out what kind of officer he is. The ship is finding out with him.

**Signature detail:** Memorizes access maps faster than anyone expects — spatial recall that looks like instinct but is actually preparation.

**Ordinary imperfection:** Writes notes in the margins of briefing documents in extremely small handwriting. Nobody can read them. He can.
