## BACKGROUND

Hale came up through fleet tactical maintenance — emitters, shields, sensor arrays, evasive geometry. He applied for the *Veil* billet because small ships make every system personal. He has served under Halden for two years and learned to read threat geometry before it becomes a briefing.

He was not surprised by the promotion. He had been doing the work.
