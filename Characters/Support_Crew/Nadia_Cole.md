## BACKGROUND

Cole learned relay work under Park. The training was partly formal, partly his habit of explaining everything twice — once technically, once in terms of what it meant for the people on the other end of the signal. She absorbed both.

She applied for the Interface transfer without being asked. Calder said yes before she finished the sentence, then told her the seat would stay open. She folded the transfer sheet and put it in her pocket.

She is going to build the station's relay infrastructure. She knows what she's doing.
