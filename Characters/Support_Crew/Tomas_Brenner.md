## ROLE

**Book One:** Senior Maintenance Technician reporting to Chief Engineer Koss. Do not retcon Book One prose.

**Book Two+ (locked):** Chief Engineer of *Kestrel Veil* after Koss's transfer to Interface. Propulsion, hull systems, maintenance leadership. Also serves as engineering mind behind the Interface station concept — Koss owns yard integration and schedule; Brenner owns what can be welded and kept alive.

Do **not** accidentally move Brenner to Interface full-time or remove him from *Kestrel Veil* without an explicit canon decision.

---

## BACKGROUND

Brenner came up through fleet maintenance depots before getting a scout billet. He has never applied for a commission and does not intend to. He trusts people who fix things, and tolerates people who command things, provided they stay out of engineering.

He transferred to *Kestrel Veil* before Book One with a maintenance certification list and zero interest in rank. Koss recognized the engineering instinct immediately. Park knows his birthday. Calder does not.

---

## PRESENCE

Almost always arrives from actual work — grease on one knuckle, pencil behind one ear, a panel half-sealed. Politics bore him. Engineering fascinates him.

**Signature detail:** Can reproduce any ship sound he has heard before — not Koss-level diagnostic genius, but uncanny mechanical mimicry he uses to mess with juniors.

**Ordinary imperfection:** Leaves tools in exactly the wrong places on purpose ("optimizing retrieval paths").

---

## CREW GRAVITY

- Solves problems before meetings finish discussing them.
- Mehta flagged the coolant relay before he had coffee; he immediately advocated for her title to match her work.
- Straightens the coffee urn lid whenever he passes it. He does not know he does this.
- First response to any new assignment or ship development: verify the engineering implication before acknowledging the politics.

---

## AUTHENTIC VOICE

Gruff, economical, verbs first; shipyard cadence; dry teasing of juniors.

**Blind-read:** sounds like metal and habit, not bridge officers.

**Oaths:** `"damn"`, `"bastard"` (tool/machinery only); invented `"shear"`.

**Romance:** straight; married shore-side (spouse unnamed).

**Never sounds like:** Calder, Reyes, Okwelu.
