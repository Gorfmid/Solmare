# DR. SERU OKWELU — CHIEF MEDICAL OFFICER, KESTREL VEIL

**Status:** Active (Book One → Book Two+)  
**Rank:** Lieutenant (Medical Corps)  
**Age:** 38  
**Reports to:** Captain (medical authority); day-to-day coordination with XO on crew rest cycles  
**See also:** `Characters/Support_Crew/support_crew.md`

---

## ROLE

Primary medical officer. Routine care, fitness certification, sleep-deprivation monitoring, emergency stabilization until relay evacuation. On a scout vessel, medical is one person — Okwelu is it.

**Book Two+ rule:** Protected recurring. Used sparingly but memorably.

---

## BACKGROUND

Okwelu trained through Fleet Medical Corps with a specialty in isolated-deployment care — environments where evacuation is delayed and the physician works without backup. Scout postings suited her: she prefers useful over prestigious.

She has served on three scout-class vessels before *Kestrel Veil*. She does not talk about the second one.

---

## PRESENCE

Appears in passageways more than on the command deck. Thessaly consults her before enforcing rest cycles. Calder forgets she exists until someone needs her — a dynamic Okwelu finds professionally irritating and personally unsurprising.

**Signature detail:** Keeps a paper notebook despite ship tablets — *"latency doesn't apply to pen and paper."*

**Ordinary imperfection:** Perpetually loses one earpiece from her diagnostic headset. The other one is always exactly where it should be.

---

## CREW GRAVITY

- Has told Calder three times his sleep fragmentation is medically unsustainable. He acknowledged all three. He changed nothing.
- Mehta reports to her on crew wellness compliance (via Solis in Book Two+); Okwelu enforces without drama.
- Cuts off apologies before they finish. Mehta has stopped apologizing to her — which Okwelu considers a small professional victory.

---

## AUTHENTIC VOICE

Measured clinical plain speech; complete sentences; no Fleet jargon unless medical; gentle firmness without raising her voice.

**Blind-read:** the adult in the room who will not argue twice.

**Oaths:** none on duty; private `"hell"` rare.

**Romance:** straight; no on-page partner Book One.

**Never sounds like:** Calder (authorize/record), Park (banter), Ortega (speed).
