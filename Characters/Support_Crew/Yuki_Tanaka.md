# YUKI TANAKA — FLIGHT OPERATIONS, KESTREL VEIL

**Status:** Active (Book One → Book Two+)  
**Rank:** Petty Officer First Class  
**Age:** 31  
**Reports to:** Senior Navigator Reyes  
**See also:** `Characters/Support_Crew/support_crew.md`

---

## ROLE

**Book One / Book Two+:** Jump sequencing support, transit checklist execution, secondary navigation monitoring, corridor data ingestion.

**Book Two+ expanded:** Shuttles, probes, launches, recoveries, local traffic. Reyes calculates; Tanaka runs sequences and catches documentation Reyes's head-visualization skips.

**Book Two+ rule:** Protected recurring. Works closely with Reyes.

---

## BACKGROUND

Tanaka transferred to *Kestrel Veil* from *Meridian Echo* with Reyes, Koss, and Park — a quiet transfer bond that doesn't need acknowledgment to hold. She applied for the posting because Reyes was the best navigator she'd worked under. She has never told him that.

She has precise habits that look like superstition from the outside and are simply competence from the inside. Her color-coded jump checklists are not explained to new crew. They do not need to be.

---

## PRESENCE

On the command deck during jump prep and exit more often than not. Speaks when spoken to on-watch; precise when she does speak.

**Signature detail:** Color-codes personal jump checklists in a system only she and Reyes fully understand. She has never needed to explain a miss.

**Ordinary imperfection:** Chronically late to off-watch meals by exactly the amount of time it takes to recheck one more thing.

---

## CREW GRAVITY

- Reyes calculates; Tanaka sequences and documents. The split is clean enough that neither has to negotiate it.
- Does not offer opinions unless asked. When asked, the opinion is worth asking for.
- Her quiet during approach prep signals more than most crew's active reporting.

---

## AUTHENTIC VOICE

Precise, soft-spoken, checklist vocabulary; speaks when spoken to on-watch; color references in private jargon with Reyes.

**Blind-read:** quiet competence, not authority.

**Oaths:** almost none; invented `"drift"` (mild).

**Romance:** straight; no partner aboard.

**Never sounds like:** Halden (jokes), Park (warmth), Calder.
