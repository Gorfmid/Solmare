## Department Heads

### Tomas "Tom" Brenner — Chief Engineer

**Rank:** Chief Petty Officer (engineering authority on scout hull; Book Two billet = Chief Engineer)  
**Age:** 44  
**Reports to:** Captain  
**Coordinates with:** Elara Koss (Interface Construction & Integration)

**Role (Book Two+):** Ship engineering authority after Koss’s transfer. Propulsion, hull systems, maintenance leadership, and hands-on construction partner for Interface. Koss owns yard integration and schedule; Brenner owns what can be welded and kept alive.

**Book One role (locked history):** Senior Maintenance Technician reporting to Chief Engineer Koss. Do not retcon Book One prose.

**Presence:** Almost always arrives from actual work. Gruff, competent, loyal to the work. Politics bore him; engineering fascinates him.

**Signature detail:** Can reproduce any ship sound he has heard before—not Koss-level diagnostic genius, but uncanny mimicry used to mess with juniors.

**Ordinary imperfection:** Leaves tools in exactly the wrong places on purpose ("optimizing retrieval paths").

**Series continuity habits (locked):** Solves problems before meetings finish discussing them. Humor from practicality, never from trying to be funny.

**Running note:** Park knows Brenner's birthday. Calder does not.

**Authentic voice:** Gruff, economical, verbs first; shipyard cadence; dry teasing of juniors. **Blind-read:** sounds like metal and habit, not bridge officers. **Oaths:** **"damn"**, **"bastard"** (tool/machinery only); invented **"shear"**. **Romance:** straight; married shore-side (unnamed spouse). **Never like:** Calder, Reyes, Okwelu.

### Dr. Seru Okwelu — Chief Medical Officer

**Rank:** Lieutenant (Medical Corps)  
**Age:** 38  
**Reports to:** Captain (medical authority); day-to-day coordination with XO on crew rest cycles

**Role:** Primary medical officer. Routine care, fitness certification, sleep-deprivation monitoring, emergency stabilization until relay evacuation. On scout vessels, medical is one person—Okwelu is it.

**Book Two+ rule:** Protected recurring. Used sparingly but memorably. Calm. Competent. Professional.

**Presence:** Appears in passageways more than on the command deck. Thessaly consults her before enforcing rest cycles. Calder forgets she exists until someone needs her.

**Signature detail:** Keeps a paper notebook despite ship tablets—"latency doesn't apply to pen and paper."

**Ordinary imperfection:** Perpetually loses one earpiece from her diagnostic headset.

**Running note:** Has told Calder three times his sleep fragmentation is medically unsustainable. He acknowledged all three. He changed nothing.

**Authentic voice:** Measured clinical plain speech; complete sentences; no fleet jargon unless medical; gentle firmness without shouting. **Blind-read:** the adult in the room who will not argue twice. **Oaths:** none on duty; private **"hell"** rare. **Romance:** straight; no on-page partner Book One. **Never like:** Calder (authorize/record), Park (banter), Ortega (speed).

### Yuki Tanaka — Flight Operations Chief

**Rank:** Petty Officer First Class  
**Age:** 31  
**Reports to:** Senior Navigator Reyes

**Role:** Jump sequencing support, transit checklist execution, secondary navigation monitoring, corridor data ingestion. **Book Two+ expanded:** shuttles, probes, launches, recoveries, local traffic. Reyes calculates; Tanaka runs sequences and catches documentation Reyes’s head-visualization skips.

**Book Two+ rule:** Protected recurring. Works closely with Reyes.

**Presence:** On the command deck during jump prep and exit more often than not. Speaks when spoken to on-watch; precise when she does speak.

**Signature detail:** Color-codes personal jump checklists in a system only she and Reyes understand.

**Ordinary imperfection:** Chronic lateness to off-watch meals by exactly the amount of time it takes to recheck one more thing.

**Running note:** Was on *Meridian Echo* with Reyes, Koss, and Park. Quiet transfer bond—doesn't push, doesn't need to.

**Authentic voice:** Precise, soft-spoken, checklist vocabulary; speaks when spoken to on-watch; color references in private jargon with Reyes. **Blind-read:** quiet competence, not authority. **Oaths:** almost none; **"drift"** (mild invented). **Romance:** straight; no partner aboard. **Never like:** Halden (jokes), Park (warmth), Calder.

### Nadia Cole — Station Relay / Communications (Interface)

**Rank:** Petty Officer Third Class  
**Age:** 29  
**Reports to:** Interface project leadership (coordinates with Halden / Walsh); remains Fleet-side counterpart to Dana Holt

**Role (Book Two+):** Transfers from *Kestrel Veil* to Interface — station relay and communications once the walls exist. Gradual path: become **Dana Holt’s primary Fleet counterpart** on the station/node side. **Park stays** as *Veil* communications / relay.

**Book One role (locked history):** Relay technician under Park. Do not retcon Book One.

**Presence (Book One / early Book Two):** Secondary relay station; knows technicians by name because Park insisted and Cole kept the habit. After Ch.4 greenlight: Interface planning rooms and yard relay buildout.

**Signature detail:** Can identify relay congestion by sound before the display flags it—learned from Park, not trained.

**Ordinary imperfection:** Collects novelty mugs from every port. Park's lost-mug problem is partly Cole's fault (she "borrows").

**Authentic voice:** Park's rhythm but quieter; technician practical; sound-before-display intuition. **Blind-read:** relay jargon without Park's social layer. **Oaths:** **"static"** with Park; **"damn"** rare. **Romance:** straight; no arc Book One. **Never like:** Ortega, Calder.

### Marcus Hale — Tactical & Defensive Systems

**Rank:** Petty Officer Second Class (Book One / early Book Two) → **Petty Officer First Class** at Ch.4 greenlight with principal tactical advisor billet aboard *Veil*. Further rank earned later — not a jump to Halden’s former Lieutenant Commander.  
**Age:** 33  
**Reports to:** Captain / XO

**Role (Book Two+):** Expanded tactical role — not only defensive hardware. Principal tactical advisor aboard *Kestrel Veil*. Threat assessment, defensive systems, evasive geometry. Provides a different perspective than Thessaly.

**Book One role (locked history):** Defensive Systems Technician reporting to Ari Halden. Do not retcon Book One.

**Name collision lock:** Not **Commander Hale** of Doctrine Bureau.

**Presence:** Appears when posture increases. Dry humor in Halden’s former orbit; with Halden transferred, humor stays dry and rarer on the command deck.

**Signature detail:** Named his favorite emitter array. Nobody else uses the name.

**Ordinary imperfection:** Falls asleep in any chair angled more than fifteen degrees from vertical.

**Authentic voice:** Dry brevity; hardware names; jokes sparingly. Thanks are short; then he goes back to the tool. **Blind-read:** tactical maintenance and survival geometry, not philosophy. **Not Ari Halden 2.0:** Halden walks worst cases out loud, archives, gallows humor, if/then briefing. Hale stays closer to the panel — emitters, workarounds, maintenance windows. Do not give him Halden’s scenario chains or pre-grief voice. **Oaths:** **"hell"** once per book max; invented **"spike"**. **Romance:** straight; no partner aboard. **Never like:** Park, Ortega, Halden.

### Sana Mehta — Life Support & Environmental Systems

**Rank:** Crewman First Class  
**Age:** 22  
**Reports to:** Chief Engineer Brenner

**Role (Book Two+):** Owns life support and environmental systems after Brenner’s promotion into CE. Atmosphere processing, water reclamation, berth environmental systems. Young but highly capable. Often solves practical engineering problems without fanfare.

**Book One role (locked history):** Life-support technician under Brenner (then Senior Maintenance). Do not retcon Book One.

**Presence:** Most likely crew member carrying a toolkit in a passageway. Koss still knows her certification status by heart when their paths cross on Interface work.

**Signature detail:** Sends weekly relay messages to her grandmother on Helion Prime without fail.

**Ordinary imperfection:** Apologizes when nothing is her fault. Park has stopped trying to fix this.

**Authentic voice:** Polite, quick apologies, practical life-support terms; young enlisted. **Blind-read:** earnest, over-responsible. **Oaths:** **"hell"** embarrassed; invented **"choke"**. **Romance:** straight; grandmother on Helion Prime, no shipboard romance. **Never like:** Halden, Thessaly, Brenner.

**Not Priya Sharma** (Lisette's bakery assistant). See `Characters/Support_Crew/Sana_Mehta.md`.

### Lena Walsh — Security / Access (Interface + ship utility)

**Rank:** Crewman Second Class  
**Age:** 24  
**Reports to:** Executive Officer Thessaly (ship); works with Commander Halden on Interface security/access throughout construction

**Role:** Secondary monitoring, cargo checks, general shipkeeping, overflow tasks. Book Two Contact Node / Interface work: security and access design. Flexible slot—competent at many things until the work finds her.

**Presence:** Thessaly's utility player. Easy to overlook until absent. Increasingly present in Interface planning rooms.

**Signature detail:** Perfect personal watch notebook Thessaly once borrowed as a scheduling reference without telling her.

**Ordinary imperfection:** Cannot whistle despite trying constantly in maintenance corridors.

**Series continuity habits (locked):** Quiet operational competence — notebook already open, already thinking, already solving. Rarely wastes words. Security is how she understands the world. Competence recognizes competence. Book Two Ch. 3: Vex notices her professionalism first (not appearance); mutual professional fascination planted, not romance on the page yet.

**Authentic voice:** Plain, helpful, slightly formal; notebook precision; rank-appropriate fleet speech. **Blind-read:** the reliable extra hand—not flashy. **Oaths:** **"damn"** under breath logging. **Romance:** straight; Book Two plants professional fascination with Commander Vex (not romance on the page yet). **Never like:** Park, Halden, Ortega.

### Felix Ortega — Science Technician

**Rank:** Petty Officer Second Class  
**Age:** 26  
**Reports to:** Senior Navigator Reyes

**Role:** Sensor array operation, sweep data ingestion, spectral analysis prep, archive cross-reference execution. Reyes interprets; Ortega runs routines and flags raw anomalies first.

**Book Two+ rule:** Still aboard. Not elevated to protected department lead. Use when sensor work needs a second voice; do not let him vanish without continuity.

## Dialogue / Presence Rules

- Support and department leads speak on the command deck when reporting or when addressed. They do not offer unsolicited strategic opinions.
- They have names, habits, and ordinary imperfections—and, Book Two+, protected cast have arcs of competence and career continuity.
- **Each must pass blind-read test:** dialogue identifiable without rank tags.
- Park and Thessaly know everyone by name. Calder is still learning the quieter ones. Reyes knows Tanaka and Ortega. Brenner knows Mehta. Halden (when present) knows Hale and Walsh.
- In crisis: same functional brevity as senior officers. Personality compresses, does not disappear.
- **Romantic orientation (default):** straight unless canon revised.
- **Language:** no profanity stronger than *hell* / *damn* / *bastard*; prefer character-specific invented oaths listed above.
