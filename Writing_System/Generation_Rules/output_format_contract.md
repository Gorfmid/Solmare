# OUTPUT FORMAT CONTRACT — BOOK ONE

All generated chapters must follow this structure exactly.

---

## FORMAT

### CHAPTER HEADER
- Chapter Number
- Title
- POV Character
- Location
- Ship Class

---

### SECTION 1 — STATUS QUO
Current operational state of:
- ship
- crew
- mission
- environment

---

### SECTION 2 — OBJECTIVE
Explicit mission directive.

---

### SECTION 3 — INCIDENT CHAIN
Chronological escalation events only.
No introspection in this section.

---

### SECTION 4 — DISCOVERY
Single most important revelation of chapter.

---

### SECTION 5 — AFTERMATH STATE
New baseline state:
- what changed
- what is now broken / uncertain
- what command believes vs reality

---

## STYLE CONSTRAINTS

- No meta narration
- No exposition dumps outside Discovery section
- No unresolved contradictions within same chapter
