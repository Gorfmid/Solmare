# CONTINUITY RULES — BOOK ONE

## CORE PRINCIPLE
The universe is consistent unless explicitly changed by a documented anomaly event.

---

## ABSOLUTE RULES

### 1. Ship Capability Consistency
Ships cannot perform actions outside their defined class specification.

Combat class hierarchy and tactical rules: `Universe/canon_and_world_rules.md`  
Combat behavior and battle scene logic: `Universe/fleet_doctrine_and_combat_logic.md`  
Crew complements and officer scaling: `Universe/Fleet/fleet_crew_and_officer_canon.md`  
Boarding combat and internal ship geography: `Universe/Fleet/boarding_combat_and_internal_ship_geography.md`  
Communication, sensors, and information warfare: `Universe/Fleet/fleet_communication_sensor_and_information_warfare_doctrine.md`  
Fleet Authority governance and command hierarchy: `Universe/Fleet/fleet_authority_governance_and_command_hierarchy.md`  
Mission designation (e.g. Scout-Class): `Universe/Technology/scout_class.md`

---

### 2. Communication Limits
If a system is marked as delayed or degraded:
- Real-time command is impossible
- Orders may conflict or arrive out of sequence

Full doctrine: `Universe/Fleet/fleet_communication_sensor_and_information_warfare_doctrine.md`

---

### 3. Anomaly Behavior Rule
Anomalies:
- must introduce contradiction or uncertainty
- cannot be immediately explained
- cannot be resolved in same chapter they appear

---

### 4. Empire Behavior Rule
Empires:
- behave according to doctrine.md
- may act inconsistently ONLY if internal conflict is explicitly introduced

---

### 5. Character Knowledge Rule
Characters only know:
- what they have been shown
- what their instruments confirm
- what command explicitly states

No omniscience allowed.

---

### 6. Technology Constraint Rule
No new technology may appear unless:
- referenced in ship specs OR
- introduced via discovery block AND validated later

---

## FAILURE MODE PREVENTION

If contradiction occurs:
- treat later data as “corrupted input”
- not earlier narrative as wrong
