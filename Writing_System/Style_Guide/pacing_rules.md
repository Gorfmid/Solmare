## THE 70/30 RULE (Ch. 6+)

Mandatory from Act II forward. Full doctrine: `the_70_30_rule.md`

**~85%** crew, mission, relationships, problem-solving, politics, exploration, action, professional life.  
**~15%** direct anomaly focus.

Anomaly = weather. Crew = story.

Each chapter (Ch. 6–10) should end with a **human victory**—competent problem solved—not merely an anomaly reveal.
