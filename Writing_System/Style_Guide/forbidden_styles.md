# FORBIDDEN STYLES

**See also:** `global_writing_principles.md` → *Romantic Interests*, *Language and Swearing*

## NOT ALLOWED
- sarcasm or humor tone
- melodrama
- poetic filler descriptions
- artificially profound prose (abstract metaphors, philosophical asides, narration wiser than POV—see `global_writing_principles.md` → *Avoid Artificially Profound Prose*)
- vague sci-fi clichés
- profanity stronger than *hell*, *damn*, or *bastard*
- slurs and vulgar sexual language
- same-sex romantic or sexual storylines for principal/significant characters (romantic interests are straight)

## CONTROLLED
- awe must be grounded in observation
- invented in-universe oaths (short, pronounceable; same severity cap as mild real-world oaths above)
